About Us – Conformity Alliance

We are team of Advocates, CA’s and CS’s having experience of more than 50 years collectively in litigations, audits, registrations and consultations in different codes binding on companies to run smooth operation in India. We specialize in providing complete, end-to-end compliance solutions under a single platform to ensure “Ease of Doing Compliance” seamless and lawful business operations across India.

Under the expert guidance of our Principal Consultant, Advocate Pankaj Kumar — a recognized authority in corporate governance and labor law — our team has delivered end-to-end compliance services to clients in industries such as Logistics, Construction, E-Commerce, Retail, Food, Fintech, Manpower, Export, and Manufacturing.

Recognizing the rapid rise of Start-Ups in India and their need for full legal compliance without the burden of high HR costs, we offer affordable services including audits, legal registrations, consultations, and litigation management. Our goal is to empower Start-Ups to achieve zero non-compliance, making them investment-ready for future funding rounds.

We bring together industry expertise, operational efficiency, and a client-first approach to support organizations in managing risk, ensuring compliance, and fostering sustainable business growth.
