Registration

Every establishment needs to get some registrations/Licences to start operations in India. It is illegal to start any kind of business without valid registration/Licence. In view of this, Proprietor/Partner/Director may be prosecuted with fine, imprisonment or both.

Registrations and applicability of Acts vary from State to State and it becomes difficult for organisations to get valid registrations/licences within prescribed time.

We serve our services to organizations for registrations/licence at the best rate and time as we have presence in all States and the Union Territories of the India.
